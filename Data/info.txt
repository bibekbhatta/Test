Temperature files for UK (e.g Stornaway, Sheffield) etc are in .txt format

Temperature files from Republic of Ireland are in .zip format

Jupyter files (with .ipynb extensions) can be used to replicate graphs for individual stations.
To run execute the Jupyter files online, click on the banner for 'binder' first and it will take a few moments for the online environment to load up. Then you can click on the respective Jupyeter file to execute the codes to produce charts.

Cleaned data on max/min temperature, rain and sun is available for all 37 UK stations.
